about me

Holly Zheng is a designer currently in Connecticut. Since 2016, they have been working on design, branding, marketing, and advertising with the Asian American Cultural Center at the University of Connecticut. When not at the Cultural Center, they can be found making prints at the University print shop or making models for 3D print.
Before that, they were taking comissions for 3D prints as part of a student-run organization, 'CADD Corp', as well as making memes and gifs of their favorite fandoms on the side.
