home

Lithographic prints on paper depicting memories and ideas of home, in which 'home' is an internal place. Coming from a difficult home situation, the place that is 'home' is 'dirty' in the eyes of others. Acceptance of this fact and a need for escapism weave to make this dilapidated fantasy landscape.
