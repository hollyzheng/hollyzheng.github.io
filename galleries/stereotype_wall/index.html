sterotype wall

Wall installment made for the Asian American Cultural Center, which focuses on popular stereotypes of Asian Americans. The main purpose of the Stereotype Wall is to initiate and engage viewers in meaningful conversation about the expectations and stigma Asian Americans commonly face in society.
